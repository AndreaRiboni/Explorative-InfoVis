How many times have you taken your food for granted?
Your food is not free and your job pays for it: time is the currency now.

This map illustrates the average amount of work time needed in each country to afford a daily healthy diet. For clarity in highlighting the disparities, I have employed a logarithmic scale.

Understanding is perceiving: select a country to observe the time needed to fill a metaphorical glass. Once filled, it represents the point at which people in that country can afford their healthy diet.
